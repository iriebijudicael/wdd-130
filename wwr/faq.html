Questions you may ask!

What rivers do we run?

We explore a variety of rivers depending on the permits we get each year. We do a lot of local trips for those that want a taste of what it is like to run some rapids.

What time and year fit this activity?

These runs can start as early as April and close at the end of the year. It all depends on the weather and water there is on the rivers to be able to have a fun and safe trip.

Is there any age to participate?

Of course, all depend on how fast the river is flowing will depend too on the minimum age. According to the season, it is generally 13 yrs old. Later in the season, it will 10 yrs old.

What should I wear

For lower body: Swimming wear (or a change of underclothing) to wear under your rafting pants and tennis shoes. These will get wet. For the upper body: Bring a long sleeved shirt to wear under your rafting jacket (it will get wet), any necessary medications (asthma inhalers, etc.) and a towel to dry off after your trip each day.
